The Byredo Eau de Parfum collection is an archive of scents, centered around five
iconic scents, crafted from carefully selected, premium ingredients sourced from nature,
capturing emotions and memories we've all experienced at least once.
Each scent, crafted from natural ingredients like ambrette, sandalwood, violet, and
vetiver, focuses on memories and stories.
